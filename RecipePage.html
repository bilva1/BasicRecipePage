Homemade Noodles Recipe

This classic chinese dish is quick and easy to make with just a few ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Noodles
  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • Veggies and tofu(optional)

Nutritional Info:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 220
Protein 7g
Fat 1g
Carbs 43g
