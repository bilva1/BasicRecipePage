Homemade Noodles Recipe

This classic chinese dish is quick and easy to make with just a few ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 200g of Noodles
  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • Veggies, Onions and tofu(optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil Water:
    • In a medium-sized pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.

  2. Add veggies:
    • If you’re adding vegetables, add them to the boiling water and cook for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender.

  3. Cook Noodles::
    • Add the noodles to the boiling water (with or without the vegetables). Cook according to the package instructions, usually about 2-3 minutes, until the noodles are soft.

  4. Add Seasoning:
    • Once the noodles are cooked, add the seasoning packet that comes with the noodles. Stir well to distribute the seasoning evenly.

  5. Serve:
    • Once everything is cooked and well-mixed, transfer the noodles to a bowl. Garnish with chopped green onions or cilantro if desired.

Nutritional Info:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 220
Protein 7g
Fat 1g
Carbs 43g
